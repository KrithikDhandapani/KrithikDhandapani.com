Fitness+

Build Date : February 2024
Category : App
Engine : Xcode
Language : Swift
Github

Building games is something that I love and am very passionate about, but I decided to expand my skills even more by tasking myself to create my first mobile app. Fitness+ is a fitness app that aims to guide users in their journey to develop their overall health. By providing users with the most efficient routes for muscle building, fat loss, power training, etc., as well as accurate progress training, Fitness+ is bound to be one of the best Fitness apps out there.
