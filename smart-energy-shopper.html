Smart Energy Shopper

Build Date : May 2023
Category : Game
Engine : Unity
Language : C#

As a student in high school learning about environmental science and engineering, I wanted to spread awareness about carbon footprints and ecocities, and so I created my VR game Smart Energy Shopper. This game was developed with the aid of the Unity game engine as well as the Oculus Quest 2 headset. The functionalities in the game including player movement, first person views, and the User interface were all programmed using C#. I presented this game to professionals in the field from Stanford and Harvard and am receiving requests from PG&E representatives to use it for their own projects.
